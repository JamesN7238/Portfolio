About Me

My Motivation

Family is my greatest source of motivation. Hearing about my parents’ hard work and how they overcame the challenges they have faced has taught me the value of resilience and how to embrace the journey, not just the destination. Their determination has inspired me to bring my engineering dreams to reality. Whether it’s designing a jet engine, building a robot, or developing an AI assistant, I find joy in turning my ideas into a process of designing and building a working project from the ground up.
