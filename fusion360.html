Fusion 360 Projects

Demo Turbofan Engine

This project is a demo CAD model of a turbofan jet engine which was designed using Fusion 360. It showcases key components such as the intake, compressor, and turbine stages with the intention of representing the airflow path and housing design of a turbofan engine. This was my first major attempt at assembling multiple mechanical systems into a unified model, with internal clearance as well as focusing on the structural layout.

Demo Inline-4 Engine

This is a CAD model of a 4-cylinder inline engine that was designed and animated in Fusion 360. The model showcases accurate designs of key components, such as connecting rods, pistons, and crank throws, along with a cutaway section to help visualize the internal motion. The animation demonstrates realistic piston-crankshaft interaction by simulating the mechanics of an internal combustion engine. This project has helped me better understand mechanical motion systems and develop skills in animation rendering and motion constraints.
